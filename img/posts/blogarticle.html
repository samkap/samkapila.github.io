Dave Grohl Keynote at SXSW 2013

I’ve always been a fan of Dave Grohl’s. Nirvana. Foo Fighters (one of the best live shows ever). Queens of the Stone Age (for that one album). Earlier this year, Grohl spoke at SXSW 2013 as a keynote speaker. It’s one of the most inspiring talks I’ve heard. He talks a lot about all the lessons he’s learned along the way and it speaks to all creatives, and for designers. And, subbing out the word “music” for “design” makes for sound (pun intended) advice.

There was no right or wrong, because it was all mine.
